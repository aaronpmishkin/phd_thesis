The thesis is based on multiple publications, several of which were joint work
with other students.
In particular, \cref{chapter:fast-optimization} is based on a paper published
at ICML 2022 with Arda Sahiner and Mert Pilanci \citep{mishkin2022convex}.
Arda provided invaluable assistance implementing one-vs-all classification
strategies for convex neural networks and also ran the experiments on MNIST
and CIFAR-10, while I developed the theoretical results and conducted all other
experiments.
The work in \cref{chapter:solution-functions} is based on a paper
published at ICML 2023 \citep{mishkin2023optimal}.
I conducted all of the research presented in this chapter with the supervision
and advice of Mert Pilanci.
Finally, the deep convex reformulations in \cref{chapter:deep-reformulations}
are unpublished work;
I proved all results therein, also under the supervision of Mert Pilanci.
This final chapter has benefited from several insightful conversations with
Sungyoon Kim.
